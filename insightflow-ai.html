FAQ

Questions answered

How does the AI analyze customer feedback? +
InsightFlow uses a fine-tuned large language model trained on millions of customer feedback samples. It identifies sentiment, emotion, topic category, urgency signals, and actionable themes — across 40+ languages — in real-time as feedback arrives.
Which data sources can I connect? +
Connect email inboxes (Gmail, Outlook), survey tools (Typeform, SurveyMonkey), review platforms (G2, Trustpilot, App Store, Google Reviews), CRMs (Salesforce, HubSpot), social media (Twitter/X, LinkedIn), and more via native integrations or Zapier.
Is my customer data secure? +
Yes. All data is encrypted at rest (AES-256) and in transit (TLS 1.3). We are SOC 2 Type II certified and fully GDPR compliant. Your customer feedback data is never used to train external AI models.
Can I export reports to PDF? +
Absolutely. Pro and Business plans include one-click PDF export for all reports, including weekly summaries, trend analyses, and custom date-range snapshots. White-label branding is available on Business plans.
Do you support multiple companies or brands? +
Yes. The Business plan includes full multi-company support, letting you manage multiple workspaces, brand profiles, and teams from a single login — perfect for agencies, enterprise groups, and holding companies.
How long does setup take? +
Most teams are fully connected and seeing insights within 15 minutes. Our guided onboarding connects your first data source, runs a historical backfill, and generates your first AI report automatically — no technical setup required.
